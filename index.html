Install Smartphone App

Access Benign Canine instantly from your home screen with native smartphone performance and fast offline operations.

💡 Step-by-Step Mobile Instructions:
For iOS iPhone & iPad
  1. Open Safari browser on your iPhone.
  2. Tap the Share button in the navigation bar.
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen .
  4. Confirm and the app icon will appear on your device home screen!
For Android & Chrome
  1. Open Google Chrome or standard browser.
  2. Tap the Install prompt at the bottom, or tap browser Menu (3 dots).
  3. Select Install app or Add to Home Screen.
  4. Confirm the prompt to install as a standalone app!
🛡️ Mobile Permissions Required:
  • Geolocation (GPS): Enabled automatically for pinpointing exposure coordinates on the GIS map.
  • Camera Access: Utilized by the AI Biometric Face Matcher for street canine recognition scans.
